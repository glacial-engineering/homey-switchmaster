SwitchMaster links any switch to any light, configured entirely from the app settings page (no devices, no Flow cards).

Press a linked switch to toggle its light. If the switch is a dial, turning it fades the light up or down by a configurable relative step (default +8% / -8%). Fading up from off turns the light on; fading to zero turns it off.

Open the app settings to add links: pick a switch and a light, and for dials set the fade-up and fade-down percentages.
