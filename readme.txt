Link any IKEA BILRESA remote or motion sensor to any light without building a single Flow by hand. Pick a remote or sensor, pick a light, choose a mode, and SwitchMaster generates and maintains the Homey Flows for you, updating them automatically whenever you change the settings.

Dual-button remotes support dimmer, on/off, or two-toggle modes; scroll wheel remotes control up to three lights per dial. Motion sensors support a normal mode and a night mode that fades brightness from sunset to midnight using your Homey's location and timezone.
